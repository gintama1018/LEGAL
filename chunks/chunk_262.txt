Representation of the Anglo-Indian Community in the House of the People.—Notwithstanding anything in article 81, the President may, if he is of opinion that the Anglo-Indian community is not adequately represented in the House of the People, nominate not more than two members of that community to the House of the People. 332. Reservation of seats for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes in the Legislative Assemblies of the States.—(1) Seats shall be reserved for the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes, 2[except the Scheduled Tribes in the autonomous districts of Assam], in the Legislative Assembly of every State 3***. (2) Seats shall be reserved also for the autonomous districts in the Legislative Assembly of the State of Assam.
